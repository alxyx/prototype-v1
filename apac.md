Asia-Pacific leads the global WBG semiconductor market, holding a 46% share in 2024, with projections to grow from USD 0.98 billion in 2024 to USD 3.92 billion by 2034 at a CAGR of 14.87%. This growth is driven by EV adoption, 5G infrastructure, and renewable energy systems.
SiC and GaN semiconductors are the dominant materials, with SiC holding 58% market share and GaN expected to grow rapidly due to its role in RF and high-frequency applications.
